Artificial Intelligence

AI is already known for its superiority in image and speech recognition, navigation apps, smartphone personal assistants, ride-sharing apps and so much more.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

RPA automates repetitive tasks that people used to do.

Edge Computing

Edge computing can be used to process time-sensitive data in remote locations with limited or no connectivity to a centralized location. In those situations, edge computing can act like mini datacenters.

Quantum Computing

Quantum Computing is a form of computing that takes advantage of quantum phenomena like superposition and quantum entanglement.

AR & VR

VR immerses the user in an environment while AR enhances their environment.

Blockchain

In the simplest of terms, blockchain can be described as data you can only add to, not take away from, or change. Hence the term “chain” because you are making a chain of data.
